# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include welcoming language, respect, constructive feedback, and empathy.

## Enforcement
Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at conduct@example.com. All complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly.

For more details refer to the full Contributor Covenant version 2.1.
